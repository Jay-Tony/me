DOG BREEDS

The dog is a domesticated descendant of the wolf. Also called the domestic dog, it is derived from extinct Pleistocene wolves, and the modern wolf is the dog's nearest living relative. Dogs were the first species to be domesticated by hunter-gatherers over 15,000 years ago before the development of agriculture.Wikipedia

Dog Breeds

The origins of the domestic dog are quite unclear. However, animal breeding for the purpose of usefulness has been happening since prehistoric times. Many dog breeds have been developed during thousands of years of domestication.
